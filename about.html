About Vila Paza

The villa that was created to make your vacations even better

Located in Sarandë, 700 m from Pasqyra Beach and 1.3 km from Manastir Beach, Vila Paza provides accommodation with free WiFi and a garden with a terrace.

Some units also have a kitchenette equipped with a fridge. Pulebardha Beach is 2.1 km from the apartment, while Butrint National Park is 8 km away. The nearest airport is Corfu International Airport, 98 km from Vila Paza
